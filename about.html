About Rentzoo

Building India's smartest equipment rental platform

We're on a mission to digitize the heavy equipment rental ecosystem — making it transparent, efficient, and accessible.

Our Story

From vision to revolution

Rentzoo Private Limited was born from a simple observation: India's ₹5 lakh crore construction equipment rental market was stuck in the past. Fragmented, offline, and riddled with inefficiencies — finding the right equipment meant days of phone calls, opaque pricing, and zero guarantees.

Our founders, Suryaprakash and Koteshwar, envisioned a future where renting heavy equipment would be as seamless as booking a ride. Together with Harshil Agarwal as Chief Financial Officer, they built India's first truly digital, full-stack equipment rental platform.

Today, Rentzoo connects verified equipment vendors with thousands of contractors, builders, and enterprises across India — offering instant booking, transparent pricing, and end-to-end support.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Transform the global rental experience

Our mission is to transform the global rental experience by connecting verified vendors with customers through a smart, secure, and user-friendly platform. We aim to simplify rentals across all categories, offering real-time access and seamless end-to-end support.

To become the world's most trusted digital rental platform — making rentals fast, reliable, and accessible for everyone, everywhere — while empowering local vendors and creating a globally connected rental ecosystem.
