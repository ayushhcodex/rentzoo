Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting heavy equipment with Rentzoo.

Which areas in Hyderabad do you deliver equipment to? +

We supply heavy machinery to all major zones within and around Hyderabad, including Cyberabad, Secunderabad, Outer Ring Road (ORR) development sites, and suburban construction hubs.

How do you guarantee the quality of the machinery? +

Every machine listed on Rentzoo is owned by a strictly vetted, verified local vendor. We enforce routine maintenance checks, and all equipment must pass stringent operational safety standards before arriving at your site.

Can I rent equipment with a specialized operator (Wet Hire)? +

Yes! We strongly recommend our Wet Hire service. Rentzoo provides highly trained, licensed operators along with the heavy machinery so your site stays safe and maximally productive from minute one.

What happens if the equipment breaks down on site? +

Zero downtime is our goal. If mechanical failure occurs, our support team will instantly coordinate with the vendor for immediate on-site repair or dispatch a rapid replacement machine at zero additional cost to you.

How do I list my own idle equipment on Rentzoo? +

We are a two-sided marketplace. Click "Register Machine" in the top menu to WhatsApp our onboarding team. We handle the discovery, booking, and payment security so you can maximize the ROI of your idle machinery.
