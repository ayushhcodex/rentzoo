Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting heavy equipment with Rentzoo.

How fast can equipment be delivered?

+

We pride ourselves on speed. For standard equipment in active service zones, we aim to deliver within 30 minutes to 2 hours of booking confirmation.

Do I need to pay a security deposit?

+

Deposits vary based on the equipment type and rental duration. Some verified corporate clients are eligible for zero-deposit rentals. Details are completely transparent before you finalize your booking.

What happens if the equipment breaks down?

+

All our vendors are verified for quality, but break downs happen. If an issue occurs, our support team will instantly coordinate a repair or replacement to minimize your downtime at no additional cost if it's a mechanical failure.

Do you provide operators with the equipment?

+

Yes, we offer both 'Dry Hire' (equipment only) and 'Wet Hire' (equipment + skilled operator) for most of our heavy machinery categories.
